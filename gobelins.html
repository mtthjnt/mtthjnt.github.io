GOBELINS

Teaching collobarative workshops

Overview
In April 2017, I had the privilege to give a masterclass at Gobelins on the topic of collaborative workshops. With a colleague, we designed the entire content and we, of course, give the masterclass in the shape of a workshop!
The idea was to make sure every participants wear the shoes of a facilitor at least once during the masterclass.
On the left side, students play the role of facilitators. On the right side, students play the roles of participants. Some students were asked to play "troublemaker profiles" (e.g. the participant who doesn't understand the value of doing this, the participant always on his/her phone or the participant who doesn't let the space for the others.
