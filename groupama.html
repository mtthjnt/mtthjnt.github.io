Groupama

Data capture made simple

Context
I worked with Wealth advisors in the context of client appointments. They are going to people‚Äôs homes to sell Wealth products. They interact with their customers in the same way that a journalist would interview someone. During the discussion, Wealth advisors take notes on paper, do simulations based on clients' answers and offer them the opportunity to subscribe products.
The objective of this project was to digitise the Wealth advisors tools (mainly on paper) on tablets. With this digitisation, we had the challenge to not break the natural of the conversation during a client meeting.
Translate expectations into design drivers
Understanding the design drivers for a service that will be provided and consumed has various implications for the design of the experience. Each driver can be either an obstacle to be surmounted or an opportunity to be pursued. When we met Gan Patrimoine they told us that they wanted something "simple", "sexy" and "playful". As they can be applied to any service, we started the project by finding the sense behind each word.
Each design driver was then turned into a poster for the first workshop
Co-creation workshop
In collaboration with Gan Patrimoine, we dive deep into the service model and work on aligning the value proposition from both the provider and customer‚Äôs point of view.¬†Then, we started to lay the foundation for the technical architecture and business requirements that will support the delivery of a compelling service experience.
Wireframes
When the joint team agreed on the detailed tasks a Wealth advisor needs to achieve, I started working on userflows through wireframes. The latter help me to go further into details about where elements should be placed on each screen.¬†(yes, I used Comic Sans for my wireframes, it allowed the clients¬†to not pay attention to fonts¬† ;)
The wireframes were then turned into a clickable prototype in InVision to test with target users. The idea of taking the opposite view to a classic form was well received and met the expectations "simple" and "playful" that Wealth advisors wanted.
We finally delivered interactions specifications to Gan Patrimoine. They are now applying their brand guidelines in the look and feel of this app as a last step before giving it to their Wealth advisors.
