Engie

Rethink the billing experience for SMEs

Foreword
The project presented after is the continuation of a first project dedicated to a heuristic analysis of the subscription process in another ENGIE entity.
Context for this project
Like everybody, owners of SMEs need an energy contract for their places. And like everybody, they don't really care about their energy providers as long as there is energy available to run their business. The only moment when they grant some of their precious time to their energy providers is when there is something unusual on their bills. And understanding energy bills (from a user's perspective) is not always that easy.   
Research
So as every design project should be, we started by meeting the users: owners of SMEs. We met accountants, lawyers, opticians, restaurant owners, carpenters, chemists, hairdressers, etc.
I co-created the interview grids and ran the different interviews.
Visualizing key insights along a user journey
We have decided to avoid the traditional deck to deliver our learnings. Instead, we have produced a poster that cover the 3 dimensions of our research: learnings from stakeholders, learnings from users and opportunities for the continuation of the project. My role on this deliverable was to select the key insights, structure the different piece of information and make sure the content will be understandable without voice over.

See the full version of the poster here

Workshop 
Then, we designed a worksop in order to make Engie stakeholders ideate around the different users' painpoints identified during our research. Just after the workshop, we wrote design drivers that helped the team score the different concepts produced around the billing exeperience. Those were written from a user perspective.
1. EFFORTLESS ACCESS
Is it easy and simple to access?
2. UNDERSTANDABLE
Is it catchable at a glance? Is the content comprehensible and to the point?
3. HELPFUL
Does it solve a problem?
4. ASSISTING
Does it unload me from some of my tasks?
5. FEEDBACK
Does it keep me informed?
6. INTIMATE
Does the service help ENGIE build an empathic and humanised relationship with me?
7. LEARNING
Does it learn from me?

8. PROACTIVE
Does it anticipate my needs?
