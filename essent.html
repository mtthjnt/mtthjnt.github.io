Essent Energy

Zero UI house moving experience

Context
Essent, one of the main energy provider in the Netherlands. The initial brief to design an omnichannel approach for their customer experience, in order to become "the undisputed customer excellence leader in the energy market".
During the discover phase we confirmed our initial hypothesis: energy is a low-effort product for customers. And we also discovered that 50% of Essent‚Äôs customers loss came from poor customer service. With this capital insight, we proposed Essent to focus first on¬†designing the basics of a satisfying customer experience.
What became clear in the workshop is that when consumers have interaction with Essent the experience is mostly negative. In the basis the negative overall experience is caused by the fact that the consumer has to adapt to the Essent processes instead of Essent adapting its processes to the consumer.
Aim for neutral experience as a basis
A provider of low-involvement products should put priority on doing all the basics right and provide a neutral to positive experience as a basis. On top of this solid basis Essent can then provide relevant additional services keeping in mind the low-involvement nature of the product. On top of the basis energy is about providing comfort and taking care of a customers‚Äô household.
One call does it all
"Call us¬†and we arrange everything for you around your move"
The original idea was built around the phone as primary touchpoint: ‚ÄúOne call does it all‚Äù. However, the service should be omni-channel, delivering the same experience through different channels. Customers have different preferences for channels and also might want or need to vary touch-points throughout the overall move process.
Wireframes
To ensure a consistent experience all channels lead the customer to the same responsive mini-site. This mini-site also serves as a simple and easy accessible to-do form as information can be added when available. The service will gently remind customers when certain actions need to be done and information needs to be added to the form.
User evaluation
The mockup was evaluated externally with customers at two Gamma stores: in Breda and Roosendaal. Next to this external evaluation also an internal evaluation was held with Essent employees outside the project.
The participants liked the perceived simplicity and easiness of the flow and urged to keep this up
Essent moves with you
The selected Minimum Lovable Product focuses on the essential part of the overall move journey and promises to make moving your energy easy and hassle-free.
